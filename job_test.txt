At CGI, we help shape the future of the UK’s most critical industries by delivering secure, mission-driven solutions that make a real-world impact. CGI was recognised in the Sunday Times Best Places to Work List 2025 and has been named a UK Best Employer by the Financial Times. Your future duties and responsibilities:

In this role, you will design, develop, and deliver software solutions for mission-critical projects, taking ownership of technical challenges and contributing to the full development lifecycle.

You will have the opportunity to innovate, enhance deployment environments, and provide support for live systems, ensuring the solutions deliver lasting value for clients.

You will collaborate with a cross-functional team, working closely with stakeholders to understand requirements and deliver practical, high-quality solutions. A problem-solving mindset and adaptability are key in supporting both new capabilities and the evolution of existing platforms.

Key responsibilities:

* Design and develop secure, scalable software solutions across the full lifecycle.
* Configure, implement, and improve existing platforms.
* Maintain deployment environments and assist with live system changes.
* Work with clients and colleagues to define and assure integrated solutions.
* Expand skills across technologies while contributing to a culture of knowledge sharing.
